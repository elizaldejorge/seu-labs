What it does

A clearer path for faculty-built tools.

SEU Labs gives promising ideas structure before they become unsupported tools. The pilot focuses on safe experimentation, not uncontrolled app deployment.

1

Submit an idea

Faculty share the problem, audience, data needs, and desired outcome.

2

Review the fit

The project is checked for usefulness, feasibility, privacy, security, and sustainability.

3

Prototype safely

Approved ideas receive a recommended path for building, testing, and documenting.

Pilot scope

Designed for useful, responsible experimentation.

The pilot helps determine which tools, workflows, support models, and review standards should guide AI-assisted development at St. Edward’s.

  • Focus on faculty-led academic and instructional use cases.
  • Use security and privacy review before broader deployment.
  • Recommend supported tools and hosting paths.
  • Document projects so they can be maintained or retired responsibly.
