Support model

Simple places to get help.

During the pilot, SEU Labs can test which support channels are most useful and sustainable.

  • Monthly meetups or office-hour style sessions
  • Shared Google Chat space or group communication channel
  • Starter guides for approved tools
  • Project documentation templates
  • Clear support request process for faculty participants

FAQ

Questions faculty may ask.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. The pilot is designed to explore how faculty can use AI-assisted coding with guidance and support.

Can the app use student data?

Any project involving student information or sensitive data needs careful review before development or launch.

What tools are supported?

The pilot will help determine the preferred tools, platforms, and support model.

What happens after the pilot?

Projects may continue, migrate, pause, or retire depending on value, risk, cost, use, and support needs.
