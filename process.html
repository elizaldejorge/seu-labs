Process

A structured path from idea to prototype.

The SEU Labs process keeps experimentation moving while making sure each project is reviewed for value, feasibility, privacy, security, and long-term support.

01

Submit a proposal

Faculty share the project idea, audience, expected value, data needs, and level of AI/coding experience.

02

Intake review

The project is discussed at a high level to understand the use case, technical complexity, and whether a prototype makes sense.

03

Security and privacy check

The team reviews data use, account needs, possible integrations, FERPA concerns, and any risk to university systems.

04

Recommended path

Approved projects receive a suggested stack, support model, documentation plan, and cost expectations.

05

Prototype and test

The project moves into a working prototype, followed by testing, documentation, launch planning, and lifecycle review.
